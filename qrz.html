Hi there

I am a 17 year-old student with an ever-growing list of hobbies. I am mainly a software developer, maintaining a plethora of projects over on GitHub. I also work with software-defined radios, do photography, and much more.

For more information on my work, and my blog, see my website.

On HF, I operate my Elecraft KX2 at 1-5W. I'll occasionally bump the power up as needed if I'm having an issue with a specific QSO. I almost exclusively operate CW so I can get some morse code practice. I operate on 20m through the day, and drop down to 40m at night.
